### Key Considerations:
- **balanceOf** and **ownerOf**: Validate that functions operate normally on non-zero addresses and accurately represent the counting and owning states.

- **approve**: Ensure the caller is either the owner or an authorized operator, who can establish the approved address mapping.

- **getApproved** and **setApprovalForAll**: Accurately check the approved mappings as per current contract state.

- **isApprovedForAll**: Correctly verify the operator approval mapping.

- **transferFrom** and **safeTransferFrom**: Handle both successful transfer and self-transfer scenarios, ensuring balances change as expected, depending on whether the source and destination are the same.

This structure complements the defined ERC-721 behavior by maintaining invariant conditions and appropriate state changes throughout the functions.
