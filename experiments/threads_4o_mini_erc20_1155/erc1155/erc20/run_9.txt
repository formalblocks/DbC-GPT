### Explanation of Postconditions:

1. **balanceOf**: Ensures that the returned balance matches the stored balance in the `_balances` mapping for the specified owner and token ID.

2. **balanceOfBatch**: Ensures that the length of the returned batch balances matches the number of owners provided and that each balance corresponds to the correct owner and token ID.

3. **setApprovalForAll**: Ensures that the approval status for the operator is updated correctly. It checks that the approval state corresponds to the expected value on success and remains unchanged on failure.

4. **isApprovedForAll**: Ensures that the returned approval status matches the stored approval status for the specified owner and operator.

5. **safeTransferFrom**: Checks that the balances of the sender and recipient are updated correctly based on the transfer, handling the case where the sender and recipient are the same.

6. **safeBatchTransferFrom**: Similar to `safeTransferFrom`, it ensures that balances are updated correctly for each token in the batch transfer, again handling the case where the sender and recipient are the same.

These postconditions ensure that the contract adheres to the expected behavior defined in the ERC-1155 standard.
