### Explanation of Postconditions:

1. **`balanceOf`**: 
   - Ensures that the balance returned matches the count of NFTs owned by the specified address.

2. **`ownerOf`**: 
   - Confirms that the owner returned for a given token ID is not a zero address and matches the mapping.

3. **`approve`**: 
   - The postcondition `_tokenApprovals[_tokenId] == _approved` ensures that the approval is set correctly.
   - The additional postcondition `_tokenOwner[_tokenId] == __verifier_old_address(_tokenOwner[_tokenId])` ensures that the owner of the token remains unchanged during the approval process.

4. **`getApproved`**: 
   - Validates that the token exists and retrieves the correct approved address.

5. **`setApprovalForAll`**: 
   - Ensures the operator approval state is set correctly for the caller.

6. **`isApprovedForAll`**: 
   - Confirms the approval state for a given operator on behalf of the owner.

7. **`transferFrom`**: 
   - Validates that the token transfer updates the ownership and the token counts correctly, handling self-transfers appropriately.

8. **`safeTransferFrom`**: 
   - Similar to `transferFrom`, but also checks for the transfer's safety, ensuring that the recipient can accept the token.

9. **`safeTransferFrom` (with data)**: 
   - Same as the previous `safeTransferFrom`, ensuring that the token transfer is safe and updates the state variables correctly.

### Addressing the Verifier's Error:
The error regarding the postcondition `_tokenOwner[_tokenId] == msg.sender` has been addressed by ensuring that the approval process does not assert that the owner is the sender after the operation. Instead, we confirm that the owner remains unchanged while setting the approval.
